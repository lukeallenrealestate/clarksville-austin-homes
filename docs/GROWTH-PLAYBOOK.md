# Clarksville Austin Homes: Organic Growth Playbook

The website is built and technically flawless. Ranking is now won off the page:
getting Google to discover and trust the domain, earning backlinks, and winning
the local map pack via Google Business Profile. This is the prioritized plan.

**Honest timeline (new domain vs portals):** indexing in ~1-3 weeks, first
long-tail rankings and a traffic trickle in ~1-3 months, meaningful lead flow in
~3-6 months. Consistency beats intensity. Work this list top-down.

---

## Priority 1 - Get indexed fast (do this week)

Google can't send traffic to pages it hasn't crawled. Speed up discovery:

- [ ] **Search Console > URL Inspection > Request Indexing** on: homepage,
      `/clarksville-realtor`, `/clarksville-homes-for-sale`,
      `/clarksville-market-report`, and the three `/insights/...` articles.
      (~10/day allowed. Do the rest over a few days.)
- [ ] **Link the new site from everything you already own that Google trusts.**
      This is the single fastest way to get a new domain discovered:
  - [ ] Google Business Profile "Website" field -> clarksvilleaustinhomes.com
  - [ ] Your email signature
  - [ ] Every social profile bio (Instagram, Facebook, X, YouTube, LinkedIn)
  - [ ] austintxhomes.co (add a link if you can edit it)
  - [ ] A link from the Fifth & West site (ask Claude to add it; same owner)
- [ ] **Bing Webmaster Tools** -> Import from Google Search Console (feeds Copilot
      and some AI answers).

## Priority 2 - Google Business Profile (fastest FREE lead source)

"Clarksville realtor" / "realtor near me" show a map pack ranked on GBP, not your
website, and it moves far faster than organic search. You already have a 5-star
profile ("Luke Allen | Realtor"). Optimize it:

- [ ] Primary category: **Real estate agent**
- [ ] Service area: **Clarksville, Austin** and **78703** (plus Old West Austin,
      Tarrytown, Old Enfield, Pemberton Heights)
- [ ] Website: clarksvilleaustinhomes.com
- [ ] Services / description: mention Clarksville, Old West Austin, off-market,
      luxury, historic homes, condos, The Clarksville Condominiums
- [ ] Add photos (the neighborhood, your listings, headshot) - GBP rewards fresh photos
- [ ] **Post weekly.** Ideas: a new listing, a market stat, an insights article,
      "just closed in 78703," a West Lynn spotlight. Each post links back to the site.
- [ ] **Reviews are the #1 map-pack ranking factor.** After every closing, ask the
      client for a Google review and to **mention "Clarksville" or "78703"** in it.
      Aim for 1-2 new reviews per month. See template below.

## Priority 3 - Backlinks and local citations (the real ranking lever)

This is what separates you from the portals over time. Quality and local
relevance beat volume. Targets, roughly easiest first:

**Directories / citations (consistent Name-Address-Phone):**
- [ ] Google Business Profile (above), Bing Places, Apple Business Connect
- [ ] Zillow, Realtor.com, Homes.com agent profiles (link the website field)
- [ ] Yelp, Nextdoor (Neighborhood Lead / Nextdoor Ads), local chamber
- [ ] Your brokerage (Austin Marketing + Development Group) site -> link your page

**Local / neighborhood relevance (highest SEO value):**
- [ ] Old West Austin Neighborhood Association (OWANA) - sponsor, contribute, or
      get listed as a neighborhood resource
- [ ] Clarksville Community Development Corporation (CDC) - partnership / mention
- [ ] West Lynn corridor businesses you work with - reciprocal links / partner pages
- [ ] Local blogs and neighborhood guides that cover Austin neighborhoods

**Press / features (slow but powerful):**
- [ ] Austin Monthly, Austin American-Statesman, Community Impact, Towers.net
      (development coverage - you sell The Clarksville, that's a story hook)
- [ ] Offer to be the "Clarksville expert" source for any Austin real estate
      reporter (your history/landmark knowledge is genuinely citable)

**Content-driven links:**
- [ ] Your original history and historic-landmark content is link-worthy. When
      someone writes about Clarksville, your pages are the best free reference -
      make sure they can find them.

## Priority 4 - Keep publishing (freshness + long-tail)

Google rewards active sites. Publish **1-2 new insights articles per month**.
Each one is a new door into the site and more for AI engines to cite. Ideas:
- Clarksville vs. Downtown Austin condos
- Selling an off-market home in Clarksville: how it works
- The Belvedere vs. Westline vs. The Clarksville: a buyer's comparison
- Clarksville property taxes and what buyers should budget
- A restored bungalow vs. new construction in Clarksville: cost and tradeoffs
- Annual "State of the Clarksville Market" report

Ask Claude to build any of these - the engine is already set up in `/insights`.

## Priority 5 - Measure (monthly, 15 minutes)

- Search Console > Performance: which queries show impressions? Which pages get
  clicks? Double down on what's gaining, deepen pages that stall after 90 days.
- Search Console > Pages: confirm pages are getting indexed (not "Discovered,
  not indexed" - if many stall, that signals a need for more links/authority).
- GA4 (once connected): traffic sources and which pages drive contact-form leads.

## What NOT to do
- Don't buy backlinks or use link farms (Google penalties).
- Don't spin up thin, duplicate pages to "target keywords." Depth wins here.
- Don't obsess over daily rank checks; this is a months-long compounding game.
- Don't fake reviews. Real 5-star reviews mentioning Clarksville are gold; fake
  ones are a TREC and Google violation.
